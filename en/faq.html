Frequently asked questions

The key information you need before choosing a tour or requesting a tailor-made activity.

Which languages are available?

Tours are available in Dutch, French and English.

Can a tour be adapted to our group?

Yes. The theme, pace, duration and storytelling style can be tailored to your group and occasion.

Where can I find the price, duration and meeting point?

For existing tours, current practical information is available through the details button. For a tailor-made tour, you will receive a personal proposal.

Are tours available for schools or companies?

Yes. Schools, companies, associations, families, groups of friends and other parties can request a tour.

Can I request a specific date?

Enter your preferred date and, if possible, an alternative in the contact form. Marie-Hélène will then confirm what is available.

How does a tailor-made request work?

Briefly describe your group, occasion and interests. Availability, route and practical arrangements will then be confirmed with you.

Another question?

Send it through the contact form and Marie-Hélène will reply personally.
